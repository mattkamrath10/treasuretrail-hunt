Root-cause and fix the broken Flash Finds feed rendering issue.

* only the “Found” badge appears
* image missing
* title/content missing
* empty card spacing

This started AFTER the Supabase schema fixes succeeded.

That means:

* uploads are persisting
* feed polling is working
* frontend rendering/state hydration is failing

GOAL

Fix Flash Finds uploads so every new upload:

* renders immediately
* shows image/title/category correctly
* never creates blank cards
* appears instantly in:

  * Home Feed
  * Flash Finds
  * any relevant live feeds

* upload payload
