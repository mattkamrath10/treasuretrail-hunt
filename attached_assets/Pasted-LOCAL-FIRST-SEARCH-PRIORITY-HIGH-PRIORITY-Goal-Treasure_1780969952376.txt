LOCAL-FIRST SEARCH PRIORITY (HIGH PRIORITY)

Goal:

TreasureTrail should prioritize helping users find items locally before showing results that require shipping.

Reason:

Many users are searching for treasures, collectibles, tools, furniture, vehicles, estate sale items, auction lots, and bargains that are often best purchased locally.

SEARCH PRIORITY ORDER

Step 1:
Search TreasureTrail listings within the user's selected radius.

Default radius:
25 miles

Allow user to change:

* 10 miles
* 25 miles
* 50 miles
* 100 miles
* Nationwide

Step 2:
Search TreasureTrail listings outside the local radius.

Display section:

"More TreasureTrail Results"

Step 3:
Search local events that may contain the item.

Include:

* Estate Sales
* Auctions
* Yard Sales
* Garage Sales
* Flea Markets
* Business Inventory

Display section:

"Nearby Events That May Have This Item"

Step 4:
Search integrated marketplace APIs.

Future:

* eBay
* Etsy
* Additional approved marketplaces

Attempt to prioritize local listings when location data exists.

Step 5:
Offer Google Shopping and Google Search fallback.

INTELLIGENT WANTED WIZARD ENHANCEMENT

When creating a Wanted Request, ask:

How far are you willing to travel?

Options:

* 10 miles
* 25 miles
* 50 miles
* 100 miles
* Anywhere

Store this preference with the Wanted Request.

MATCHING SYSTEM

When new listings are created:

Prioritize notifications in this order:

1. Matching listing inside user's travel radius.
2. Matching listing outside radius but still local.
3. Matching listing requiring shipping.

Notification examples:

🔥 A matching guitar was listed 8 miles away.

🔥 A matching guitar was listed 32 miles away.

📦 A matching guitar is available with shipping.

DEMAND INTELLIGENCE

Track not only item demand but local demand.

Examples:

* 18 users within 25 miles are looking for guitars.
* 12 users within 50 miles are looking for Pokémon cards.
* 7 users within 25 miles are looking for riding lawn mowers.

This data may later be used for:

* Seller insights
* Market heat maps
* Local demand indicators
* Recommended listings
* Event planning

USER EXPERIENCE GOAL

TreasureTrail should become:

"Find it nearby first."

Only expand to shipping-based results when local opportunities do not exist.

This local-first strategy should apply to:

* Searches
* Wanted Requests
* Notifications
* Recommendations
* Marketplace integrations
* Future AI-assisted matching
