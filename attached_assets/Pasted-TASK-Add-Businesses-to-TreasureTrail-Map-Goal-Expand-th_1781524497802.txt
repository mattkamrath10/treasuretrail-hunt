Phase 3 - AI Assisted Import

Allow users to create businesses using:

A. Manual Entry

User fills out form manually.
B. Business Card AI Import

User uploads photo of business card.
AI extracts:

Business name
Address
Phone
Website
Email
C. Website Import

User pastes website URL.
AI extracts publicly available business information and pre-fills fields.
D. Facebook Page Import

User pastes Facebook business page URL.
AI extracts available business information and pre-fills fields.
Workflow:

User chooses import method.
AI extracts available information.
User reviews and edits.
User submits listing.
Listing appears on map.
