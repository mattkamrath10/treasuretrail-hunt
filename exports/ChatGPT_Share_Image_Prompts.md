# TreasureTrail — Social Share Image Prompts (for ChatGPT Plus)

These make the **4 custom link-preview images** that show when you share these
pages in iMessage, Facebook, etc.:

1. **Discovery / Home** page
2. **Blog** page
3. **Events** page
4. **Flash Finds** page

Generate them in **ChatGPT Plus** (uses OpenAI image generation, not your Replit
usage).

---

## HOW TO USE (read first)

1. **Paste the STYLE BLOCK below once** at the start of a new ChatGPT chat and send it.
   This keeps all 4 images looking like one cohesive set.
2. Then **paste the 4 prompts one at a time** (one per message). After each image,
   say "next" and paste the next prompt.
3. If ChatGPT asks about size, choose **landscape / 16:9 (wide)**. (These are wide
   banner images, never square.)
4. **Download each image** and rename the file to the exact **"Save as"** name listed
   under each prompt (for example `og-blog.png`). The name is how I match each image
   to the right page.
5. When you have all 4, **send them back to me here** and I'll optimize them, upload
   them, and wire each one to its page automatically — no further work for you.

> Tip: If an image accidentally contains text or letters, just reply "remove all text
> and regenerate." The style block already asks for no text — the page title is added
> separately, so the picture must stay clean.

## THE 4 PROMPTS

### 1. Discovery / Home
Create the banner image: A beautiful overhead flat-lay of a curated treasure-hunting collection on a warm wooden surface — vintage cameras, old books, a brass clock, ceramic vases, costume jewelry, a few small antique toys and collectible cars, arranged invitingly with breathing room around them, warm golden light. The "discover something for everyone" hero shot.
**Save as:** `og-discover.png`

### 2. Blog
Create the banner image: A cozy, inviting treasure-research scene — an open blank notebook (no writing) and a vintage magnifying glass resting beside a small grouping of collectibles (an old camera, a few antique coins, a ceramic figurine) on a warm wooden desk, soft window light, shallow depth of field. Evokes guides, tips, and learning.
**Save as:** `og-blog.png`

### 3. Events
Create the banner image: A welcoming outdoor estate/garage sale event scene on a tree-lined California street — folding tables neatly arranged with antiques, lamps, books, vintage furniture and dishware, a blank A-frame sign at the curb (no lettering), warm bright morning sunlight, a community-gathering feel (but no people).
**Save as:** `og-events.png`

### 4. Flash Finds
Create the banner image: A single standout treasure in a bright spotlight moment of discovery — a rare vintage collectible (for example an antique camera or a small collectible car) sitting on a warm surface with a soft glowing highlight and a sense of excitement and value, shallow depth of field, the rest softly blurred. Evokes "look what I just scored."
**Save as:** `og-flash-finds.png`
